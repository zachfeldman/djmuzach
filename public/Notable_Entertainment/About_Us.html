About Notable Entertainment

Music

Homegrown.

Notable Entertainment Music began with a young man and a dream. After attending countless Bar Mitzvah’s, Sweet 16s, and Weddings, he was inspired to pursue the art of playing music for the entertainment of others. Quickly following came the idea to obtain a knowledge of DJ-ing that could turn that pursuit into a job. 6 years later, clients all around New Jersey and New York keep the company busy and running full time.

Reasonable rates.

As a student-run business, Notable Entertainment is available for most weekend events. If we can book your event, we will work hard to try to deal with any budgetary constraints you may have. It is our guarantee that an effort will be made to attempt to charge a fair price to all customers. Notable Entertainment prides itself on treating the customer with respect and courtesy.

About the owner

My name is Zach, and I am currently a Music Business major at New York University, based in Greenwich Village. I’ve been into music ever since the 4th grade when I picked up my first instrument: the trombone! Although my taste in instruments has changed to a pair of DJ decks and my guitar, I still have the same passion for music that I did then. I love DJing because I’m able to apply that passion to each and every party I play at, and hopefully make a lot of people happy! Please never hesitate to contact me if you have any questions about me or the company.
