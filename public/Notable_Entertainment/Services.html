Services

We provide three basic services. Please contact us for a rate quote on any service!


  1. 1.DJ Service - Per hour rates


Music for your party, from simple background to full on dance party, is provided. Also included upon request is an amazing light show, guaranteed to give your party that “club” feel if desired. If your event is being held outside, our 100 square foot party tent protects our rig from damage in the event of inclement weather.


  1. 2.Live Sound Reinforcement - Per hour rates


Let us take care of live sound at your next event, whether it be a conference or a concert! Our equipment and the ears of an expert mixer will come together for a full and professional mix.


3. Equipment Rental - Daily rates per piece of equipment


By request, we rent out PA systems and some lighting. Contact us if you’re interested in renting any piece of standard DJ equipment or lighting.
